ABOUT GROCERY SHOP MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

The main aim for developing this project where customer can purchase and order groceries online.The system is very useful for customer can easily buy the grocery products from home through the internet.The system reduces lot of work load for customer as well as owner. By this system online grocery shopping the product is directly delivered to customer address.The system functionality of Products and orders is stored on server side in a web service.This project provides a lot of features to manage the products in very well manner.This project contains a lot of advance modules which makes the back end system very powerful.

Features of Grocery Shop Management System

  • Software manage your purchase order automatically, no need to pick copy and pen. It removes your cumbersome schedule and provides an easy and accurate approach.
  • Automatically removes product from the stock on their expires.
  • Can find the alternate products according to types , Generics formula wise etc.
  • It shows about the profit and loss of every product.
  • Gives details about the products storages.
  • Reduces manpower and saves time.

Modules of Project

  • User Management
  • Client and Vendor Management
  • Accounting Management
  • Transection Management
  • Products expires Management
  • Tax Management
  • Kit Creation
  • Suppliers Wise Purchase Order
  • Inventory Management
  • Security Management
  • MIS Reports
